Rules

Great Battle is a free web card game based on the Marvel Universe.


Before starting the game, you need to build your own deck of eight cards. Each card can be of two types, a spell and a creature.


A spell is a card that has an energy cost and a special effect.


A creature is a card that has a cost, health, and attack power.


The goal of the game is to neutralize all the opponent's creatures or kill the hero.
With each turn, the amount of available energy will increase by one, which will allow you to play stronger cards.
During the course, you can play cards from your hand for energy, the creature that was put on the field cannot
immediately attack, but can only at the beginning of your next turn.


At the start of each of your turns, you will
draw a random card from the draw pile represented by your deck.


On your turn, you can attack enemy creatures. Or you can attack the hero in this case,
there will be no attack on your creature.



No more than 30 seconds are given per move. If you do not complete the move after
this time, the move will automatically pass to the enemy.



Good luck and may the smartest win.
